"Oh, I got to deal with this now. How in the world does this work!"

I land on a primitive insight, a real Arachne's thread.<br>
Forced out of my comfort zone,<br>
I have no way but forward.

"Woah that was it? Not too bad, could have been much worse!"

But in rare situations of stillness and silence between these magical moments<br>

A void too large to fill, too large to ignore<br>
A void encompassing the nature of reality
